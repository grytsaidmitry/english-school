Our Faculty

Expert Educators.
Exceptional Results.

Every instructor at Premier Language Institute holds internationally recognized teaching qualifications and brings deep subject expertise to every lesson.

Co-Founder / Academic director / Language instructor
Anthony L. Hernández

Co-Founder and Academic Director of Premier Language Institute, where he also serves as an English instructor. A retired U.S. Army veteran and international Education and Training Consultant with over eight years of experience, brings global leadership and academic expertise to the classroom. Originally from Southern California and now based in Prague, he holds degrees from the University of Costa Rica and Georgia Southern University, with studies in Spanish Literature, French, and International Affairs. Experience as a tactics/ training contractor supporting NATO soldiers and military personnel across Eastern Europe, Latin America, and the Middle East, combining real-world experience with a passion for language education.

Co-Founder / Language instructor / Music teacher
Ori Huberman

Co-Founder and Language Instructor at Premier Language Institute, where he also teaches music. Born in the United States and raised in Israel, Ori studied Music Education and built a dynamic career in Los Angeles, living there for over 20 years. During that time, he worked extensively as a professional musician and music teacher, performing at numerous concert venues and touring across the United States. Relocated to Prague and quickly transitioned into teaching English while continuing to share his passion for music. His international background and creative spirit bring energy, cultural depth, and authenticity to every classroom he leads.
