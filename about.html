Our Story

Founded on the Belief That
Excellence Is for Everyone

Premier Language Institute was established in 2009 by Dr. Margaret Clarke, a Cambridge-trained linguist with a clear and urgent conviction: that access to truly excellent English language education should not be reserved for a privileged few. After more than a decade teaching at leading universities across the United Kingdom and Australia, Dr. Clarke returned home determined to build something different ‚Äî an institution where rigour and warmth existed not in opposition, but in concert.

What began as twelve students and two teachers in a converted townhouse has grown, over fifteen years, into one of the most respected language institutions in the region. Today, Premier Language Institute serves learners from more than 45 countries, employs over 30 credentialed educators, and has guided more than 12,000 graduates toward their professional, academic, and personal goals.

Our growth has never diluted our founding standards. Classes remain small and purposefully intimate. Every student is assessed individually and placed in the programme best suited to their level and ambitions. Every teacher is selected not only for their qualifications ‚Äî though these are exceptional ‚Äî but for their genuine commitment to student success.

At Premier Language Institute, we measure our success not in enrolment figures but in outcomes: exam scores achieved, promotions earned, universities entered, and the quiet, profound confidence our graduates carry with them long after they leave our classrooms. That is the Premier standard ‚Äî and it is one we hold ourselves to every single day.

"Language is not merely a tool of communication ‚Äî it is the architecture of thought. At Premier Language Institute, we do not simply teach English. We expand what is possible for every person who studies here."
‚Äî Dr. Margaret Clarke, Founder & Principal
